# Safety Snapshot

**Safety Snapshot** is a simple mobile-first workplace safety inspection tool for everyday safety checks, findings, and corrective actions.

It runs as a static web app from a single `index.html` file. No login, backend, database, or paid service is required.

## Live App

https://tanercetinkaya-hse.github.io/safety-snapshot/

## Purpose

Safety Snapshot is designed to help users carry out quick workplace safety checks in a simple and practical way.

It can support general safety walkthroughs in everyday workplace environments such as offices, warehouses, workshops, showrooms, contractor work areas, and other operational spaces.

## Key Features

* Mobile-first inspection interface
* 15 safety checklist modules
* 5 questions per checklist module
* Option to use or skip each checklist
* Yes / No / N/A responses
* Compliance score and progress indicators
* High / Medium / Low finding counts
* Suggested corrective actions for findings
* Corrective action tracking with risk, owner, due date, and status
* Multiple inspections saved locally in the browser
* Auto-save on every change
* DOCX report export
* Email summary option
* Print / PDF option
* Light and dark mode support based on device settings

## Checklist Modules

The app includes the following checklist modules:

1. Housekeeping
2. Walking Surfaces & Access
3. Fire Safety
4. Emergency Readiness
5. First Aid & Welfare
6. Electrical Safety
7. PPE
8. Plant, Tools & Equipment
9. Working at Height
10. Manual Handling & Ergonomics
11. Chemicals & Hazardous Substances
12. Vehicle & Pedestrian Safety
13. Contractor / Maintenance Work
14. Environmental Controls & Waste
15. Safety Communication & Reporting

## How to Use

1. Open the app.
2. Enter basic inspection details.
3. Select the checklist modules relevant to the workplace.
4. Answer the inspection questions.
5. Add corrective actions where needed.
6. Export, email, or print the inspection report.

## Data and Privacy

Inspection data is stored locally in the user’s browser.

The app does not use accounts, external databases, analytics, tracking, or cloud sync.

If browser storage is cleared, saved inspections may be lost.

## Disclaimer

This tool is for general support in workplace safety inspections. It does not replace legal compliance assessment, competent professional judgment, or employer-specific procedures.
